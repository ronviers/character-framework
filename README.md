# Character — a unification primer

> **Character** is a phenomenology of driven-dissipative systems: a *measurement discipline* —
> one coordinate system that forces a set of established non-equilibrium results to read together.
> It is an effective description, not a claim to fundamentality.

This document is a **map, not the content**. Each claim below is stated once and linked to the
canonical document that carries it; read the links for the derivations and the receipts. The
framework introduces **no new mathematical object** — its contribution is the *reading* and the
*falsifiers*, and every borrowed result is named in the import ledger.

The canonical corpus currently lives in the working repository
[`mpa-atlas`](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas) at the frozen snapshot **`character-v0.1`**;
the links below point there. A Character-foregrounded rewrite of that corpus is in progress and will
migrate into this repository.

*Lineage: Character descends from work developed under the working handle **MPA** (Metastable
Propositional Algebra). The framework deflated over its history, by its authors' own hand — from a
four-valued logic to this substrate-neutral, falsification-disciplined effective description. The
deflation is the credential, not the embarrassment.*

## 1 · The one move

Take the operator algebra of the Boolean ring and deform it at finite resolution. The result is a
coordinate system in which fluctuation–dissipation, cycle thermodynamics, signed-graph balance,
exceptional points, and normally hyperbolic manifolds are not five subjects but **five readings of
one dissipative structure**. The novelty is *compositional* — new edges between imported nodes,
never new nodes. What Character owns is the reading and the falsifiers; the machinery is borrowed
wholesale, and each borrowing is named.

→ the claims: [character_engine.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_engine.md)
· what is borrowed: [character_prior_art.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_prior_art.md)

## 2 · The through-line

- **One character.** A *character* is a sustained, protected non-equilibrium steady state — the
  circulating structure a driven-dissipative system holds against decay.
  → [character.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character.md)
- **The space of characters.** Read each quantity of a single character as structure on the manifold
  of *all* characters: the broken-detailed-balance affinity becomes a coordinate, the deformation
  algebra a tangent space.
  → [character_manifold.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_manifold.md)
- **Emergence under coupling.** Character-space is closed under coupling, so composition folds back
  onto it: a composite can carry protected circulation **neither part had**.
  → [character_composite.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_composite.md)

The route to both lifts ran through the framework's **two-frame fluctuation–dissipation
treatment** — the construction that distinguishes a system's *external* (probe) frame from its
*intrinsic* (self-probe = affinity) frame. Working that treatment is what surfaced the lift and its
iteration; the space of characters and emergence-under-coupling came out of it, not from positing
them.
→ [character_fdr_treatment.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_fdr_treatment.md)

## 3 · How it is kept honest

Every load-bearing claim carries a pre-registered falsifier. Provisional work is quarantined in a
maturity ledger and crosses into the canonical core only on a recorded trigger — a real
cross-substrate instance, or a forced-not-fitted derivation. Nothing is asserted as settled that has
not earned in.

→ maturity ledger: [character_frontier.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_frontier.md)
· derivations: [character_receipts_engine.md](https://github.com/ronviers/mpa-atlas/blob/character-v0.1/framework/character_receipts_engine.md)

## 4 · What is earned, and what is not

Character is **synthetically vindicated** throughout and **world-vindicated at one instance**: a
published dissipative DNA system — a fuel-driven strand-displacement network — instances the
composite layer's central claim (coupling mints a protected, sustained circulation neither part
carried), confirmed against the full non-linear model, with stated idealizations. That is **one**
real substrate; more are owed.

The framework's deepest target — the self-sustaining **alive loop** (a character among its own
drivers; recursive protected circulation, not mere persistence) — is named and **parked, not
claimed; that work continues.**

A small, true, falsifiable claim, with one real receipt and an open frontier: that is the whole
posture.
